Privacy Policy – Content Cleaner

Last updated: 2025-12-28

What the extension does

Content Cleaner hides or blurs unwanted feed items on supported websites based on user-selected filters and keywords.

Data the extension uses

  • On-page text (local only): The extension reads text visible on supported pages (e.g., captions/titles) to decide whether to hide/blur an item. Processing happens locally in your browser.
  • User settings: The extension stores your preferences (enabled packs, keywords, hide/blur mode) using Chrome extension storage so settings persist.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect or store your name, email, passwords, messages, or payment information.
  • We do not sell user data.
  • We do not send page content to external servers.

Data sharing

No user data is sold or shared with third parties.

Data retention

Settings remain stored in your browser until you remove the extension or clear extension storage.
